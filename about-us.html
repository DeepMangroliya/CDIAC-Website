About Us

Vision

To Prepare Automation Engineering Experts for Industry and Business.

Mission

To provide state of the art training programs to budding engineers on Automation and Control. For this purpose the centre of excellence on Automation shall develop learning resources and materials, and carry out the activities including organizing value addition programs, designing industry based curricula or custom design programs.

Quality Statement

The centre is committed to design and implement meaningful programs with quality learning resources and methods, aiming towards excellence and relevance to industry and business

Activities at the Centre

We offer value addition training to students and budding engineers to create opportunities for Automation Engineers across different industry verticals, to extend resources to students to learn PLC/DCS/SCADA/HMI/Drives/Wireless/Embedded & Robotics based Automation systems for projects, seminars to improve the employability and to promote innovative ideas on Automation in order to address the real life issues on Automation Also, we creating learning resources on Automation and Control, Design and development of Automation curricula as per industry needs, Offering quiz competitions for students or learning engineers, Certification programs leading to postgraduate diploma in Automation; to Create awareness about International Standards of Automation.

Achievement

Our student got selected in Automation & relevant Industry. 400+ students & 50+ faculty/Industry Delegates has completed Training within six month on Industrial Automation & Embedded System
